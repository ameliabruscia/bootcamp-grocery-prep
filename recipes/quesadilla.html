Quesadilla

Servings

1

Tbh you should already know how to make this

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 flour tortilla
  • 1 grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 avocado

    • Instructions:

  • Place tortilla on pan over medium heat
  • Sprinkle cheese on tortilla and wait for it to melt
  • Fold tortilla in half and make sure torilla is crisp on both sides
  • Remove from pan and serve with Avocado and salsa
