BLT

Objectively the best type of sandwich

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Avocado
  • Mayonaise

    • Instructions

  • Toast sourdough bread
  • Cook bacon in frying pan until crispy
  • Slice tomato and avocado
  • Put bacon, lettuce, tomato, and avocado on bread
  • Add mayonaise and enjoy!
