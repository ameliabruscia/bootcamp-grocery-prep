Breakfast Burrito

I have yet to find a breakfast food

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Tortilla
  • Bacon
  • Tater tots
  • Mexican blend cheese
  • Avocado
  • Salsa

    • Instructions

  • Heat tater tots in overn at 350
  • Cook bacon in frying pan until crispy
  • Cook scrambled eggs and sprinkle cheese on top
  • Dice onions
  • Heat tortilla
  • Put bacon, eggs, onions, tater tots, and salse in tortilla
  • Slice avocado and add to tortilla
  • Wrap up tortilla and enjoy!
