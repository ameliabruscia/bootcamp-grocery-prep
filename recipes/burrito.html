Breakfast Burrito

Best breakfast food around

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tortilla
  • 2 strips of bacon
  • 1 bag of tater tots
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 avocado

    • Instructions

  • Heat tater tots in overn at 350
  • Cook bacon in frying pan until crispy
  • Cook scrambled eggs and sprinkle cheese on top
  • Dice onions
  • Heat tortilla
  • Put bacon, eggs, onions, and tater tots in tortilla
  • Slice avocado and add to tortilla
  • Wrap up tortilla and enjoy!
